01 — Economic Impact

Strengthening the local economy & reshaping competition

StepGuard's predictive model generates direct savings for operators, restructures the German maintenance market, and seeds local job creation.

11 Sustainable Cities
"A single emergency escalator repair can cost up to €80,000 in materials alone StepGuard's ROI is realised from the very first prevented breakdown."
🏗️
Savings for operators

Public transport operators, airports and shopping centres benefit from lower downtime costs and fewer emergency call-outs savings reinvestable directly into local infrastructure.

⚙️
OEM market disruption

Dominant OEMs (Schindler, KONE, ThyssenKrupp) earn large revenues from reactive maintenance. StepGuard compresses that cycle, incentivising the entire sector to innovate.

🏭
Local supply chain

StepGuard uses IDS cameras produced in Obersulm, Germany directly supporting domestic hardware manufacturing and strengthening the German industrial supply chain.

👷
Job creation

Phase 3 scale-up will require a German service centre, generating direct local employment. Indirect effects include growing demand for data-literate maintenance technicians.

🤝
02 — Social Impact

Safer cities, Fairer workplaces, and an Inclusive infrastructure.

Escalators are a critical infrastructure need for elderly and mobility-impaired users. StepGuard's predictive model directly improves public safety and accessibility.

11 Sustainable Cities & Communities
On 12 March 2026, over 50 escalators broke down simultaneously at Berlin Central Station a direct impact on thousands of commuters, wheelchair users, and the elderly.
♿

Public safety & accessibility

Escalators are critical infrastructure for elderly and mobility-impaired users in metro stations and shopping centres. However, escalator reliability in Germany is currently below industry standards.

🔧

Impact for maintenance technicians

With StepGuard, the role of maintenance technicians is helped tremendously shifting from reactive emergency work to planned predictive maintenance. The system creates demand for skilled roles.

🔍

Digital inclusion

Because StepGuard will be used by large public transport operators, the technology is designed to be understandable to maintain social legitimacy. The platform is user-centric by design.

🌿

04 — Ethical Impact

Responsible AI, Human oversight, and Accountable by design.

StepGuard's four-level alarm system has real operational consequences. That is why accountability and human control are foundational design requirements not afterthoughts.

16 Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions
17 Partnerships for the Goals
A Level 4 alert will result in an escalator shutdown to prevent total collapse but the system will only ever suggest action. Human intervention always has the final say.
🤖

Transparency & accountability in AI decision-making

The four-level alarm system StepGuard uses has real operational consequences. A Level 4 alert will result in an escalator shutdown to prevent total mechanical failure, meaning the system requires full explainability.

📋

EU AI Act compliance January 2027

Because StepGuard's AI vision component contributes to a safety-critical function in public infrastructure, it must be assessed by a notified body before the EU AI Act's January 2027 deadline.

🏢

Ethical responsibility to the maintenance workforce

StepGuard does not aim to eliminate reactive maintenance jobs it improves them. Shifting from dangerous emergency call-outs to planned predictive maintenance gives technicians safer working conditions.

🌐

Responsible global diffusion

As StepGuard expands into further European markets, Cboost commits to applying the same ethical minimum conditions everywhere: human oversight in all AI-triggered actions, and transparent communication.
